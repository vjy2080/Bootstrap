About

Company Profile

A trusted global tech-enabled services partner for governments and citizens, BLS International is amongst the top three global players in this domain. The company is a preferred partner for Embassies and Governments across the world, having an impeccable reputation for setting benchmarks in the domain of visa, passport, consular, e-governance, attestation, biometric, e-visa and retail services. It also provides citizen services to state and provincial governments across Asia, Africa, Europe, South America, North America & Middle East.
With over 18 years of experience and having established itself as a partner of choice for client governments worldwide, BLS is also recognized as ‘Best under a Billion Company” by Forbes Asia and is amongst Fortune India’s Next 500 companies. The company has a robust strength of over 20,000+ employees and associates who operate from more than 50,000 Global centers globally and have helped process over ~83 million applications till date.
Our journey to its current market standing has been remarkable. The first contract was that of providing visa processing services to the Portuguese Embassy in New Delhi in the year 2005 and now the operations have scaled up across 62 nations.
